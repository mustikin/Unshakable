# Unshakable You — Identity Reset Mini-Course

Free interactive mini-course based on the *Unshakable You* framework. Eight pillars. Guided exercises. Certificate included.

**→ [Open the Course](https://mustikin.github.io/Unshakable)**

---

## What This Is

You start something. Two weeks in, you're back to zero. This isn't a discipline problem — it's a **foundation problem**.

This mini-course walks you through the **8-Pillar Identity Reset Framework** that explains why you keep abandoning the same things, and gives you a system to actually break the cycle.

- **8 interactive pillars** — structured learning, not just theory
- **Guided exercises** — reflection questions that matter
- **Your answers saved locally** — nothing sent unless you opt in
- **Certificate of completion** — when you finish all 8 pillars
- **No signup required** (optional email for certificate)

Built for people who've read the *Unshakable You* book or want a structured reset experience.

---

## Get Started

1. **Click the link above** or open `index.html` in your browser (works offline, works on any device)
2. **Go through the 8 pillars** at your own pace
3. **Do the exercises** — they actually matter
4. **Get your certificate** when you complete all 8

That's it. No accounts, no installs, no complexity.

---

## What You'll Learn

The 8 pillars break down:
1. **See It** — Notice your repeating pattern
2. **Name It** — Give it language
3. **Shape It** — Understand where it comes from
4. **Live It** — Build the replacement
5. **Protect It** — Guard your progress
6. Plus three more that bring it all together

Each pillar has guided exercises to apply the framework to your own life.
